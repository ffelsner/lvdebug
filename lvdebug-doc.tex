\section{Documentation}

When you load the package \texttt{lvdebug} in your \LuaLaTeX\ document, \LaTeX\ will highlight boxes, penalties, glues and kerns in the PDF. This package requires you to process the document with \LuaLaTeX.

\begin{verbatim}
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{lvdebug}

\listfiles
\setlength\textwidth{300pt}
\setlength\textheight{10cm}

\begin{document}

A wonderful serenity has taken possession of my entire soul, like these sweet 
mornings of spring which I enjoy with my whole heart. I am alone, and feel the 
charm of existence in this spot, which was created for the bliss of souls like 
mine. I am so happy, my dear friend, so absorbed in the exquisite sense of mere 
tranquil existence, that I neglect my talents.


\bgroup\fontsize{30}{34}\selectfont
\centerline{\TeX}
\egroup

\vbox{\strut Hello}\kern .5cm\vbox{\strut World}

\[ e=mc^2 \]

\includegraphics[width=4cm]{cow}
\end{document}
\end{verbatim}
